Nurse Practitioner Joins Health Center

December 7, 2012


The Vermillion-Parke Community Health Center (VPCHC) would like to welcome Family Nurse Practitioner, Mary Davidson, FNP-C.

Mary is a 2008 graduate of Indiana Wesleyan University, where she earned her master’s degree from the Family Nurse Practitioner program.

Mary has worked in a variety of clinical settings, where she has gained much experience and opportunities to provide care to many patients. Her experience includes working as a registered nurse at Union Hospital Pain Clinic, Wabash Valley Surgery Center, Clarian West Medical Center, in various departments such as critical care, intensive care unit (ICU), medical/surgical, and emergency room. Mary has also worked in the Union Hospital Emergency Room as a registered nurse and then as a family nurse practitioner.

Mary will be the primary medical provider at the VPCHC Cayuga health center. Mary’s schedule will allow consistent, continuity of care for patients that receive medical care and treatment at the health center. She excited to join the VPCHC team and looks forward to serving the community and providing the best care possible.

The Cayuga health center is also offering walk-in appointments Monday through Friday, from 9:00 a.m. to 11:00 a.m. and 1:00 p.m. to 3:00 p.m. If you are sick or not feeling well, visit the health center during the walk-in appointment times, or call (765) 492-9042.

VPCHC is a Federally Qualified Health Center (FQHC) dedicated to providing comprehensive primary health care and related services for ALL individuals and families. VPCHC provides primary and preventive health care services, along with behavioral health services to the citizens of Vermillion and Parke counties, and surrounding communities.

The VPCHC Cayuga health center is open Monday - Friday, from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Contact the Cayuga office at (765) 492-9042 or 114. N. Division St., Cayuga, IN 47928.
