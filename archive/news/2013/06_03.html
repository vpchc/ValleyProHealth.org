Behavioral Health Services Expanding

June 3, 2013


The Vermillion-Parke Community Health Center (VPCHC) is expanding its behavioral health care services to provide more availability for patients at its health centers.

Behavioral health care focuses on the ability to adjust and cope with the stress of life. Behavioral health is necessary to personal well-being, family life, and personal relationships.

“We are excited to be expanding our services, to better meet the behavioral health needs of our surrounding communities,” stated Dr. Julia Wernz, Psychologist and Behavioral Health Director at VPCHC.

VPCHC offers behavioral health care services at its locations in Clinton, Cayuga and Bloomingdale. Services include child/adolescent counseling, family counseling, crisis counseling, and testing or assessments (upon referral).
