Health Center Begins Saturday Clinic for Acute Care

October 5, 2016

Valley Professionals Community Health Center is making it even easier for the community to get well soon. The health center will now offer Saturday Clinic services, beginning Saturday, October 8th, at the Valley Professionals’ Clinton location at 777 S. Main Street, Suite 100. The Saturday Clinic will be open from 9:00 am until 1:00 pm for acute, walk-in visits. Come see the caring professionals in your community with cold, congestion, cough, fever, sore throat, rash, vomiting, flu, earache, or similar symptoms.

Individuals do not have to be a current patient of Valley Professionals to receive primary care ser¬vices at the Saturday Clinic. All individuals and families are welcome. Valley Professionals accepts Medicare, Medicaid, Marketplace and commercial insurance, self-pay, and offers sliding fee scale based on family size and income.
