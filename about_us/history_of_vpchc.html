History of VPCHC

2008

Vermillion-Parke Community Health Center establishes it's first clinic in Clinton.

2009

New Vermillion-Parke Community Health Center clinic opens in Cayuga.

2012

The Mobile School-Based Health Center (MSBHC) begins operating, serving schools throughout Vermillion and Parke counties.

2013

January
New Vermillion-Parke Community Health Center clinic opens in Bloomingdale

September
VPCHC received recognition through the National Committee for Quality Assurance (NCQA) as a Patient Centered Medical Home with a Distinction in Patient Experience Reporting.

2014

February
Vermillion-Parke Community Health Center changes it's name to Valley Professionals Community Health Center.

November
Terry Warren named CEO of Valley Professionals Community Health Center.

2015

June
New Valley Professionals Community Health Center clinic opens in Terre Haute.

August
New Valley Professionals Community Health Center clinic opens in Crawfordsville.

2016

April
The Valley Professionals Cayuga clinic relocates to the former Speedco building on Park Street in Cayuga.

May
Valley Professionals receives a one million dollar grant from HRSA(Health Resources & Services Administration) for expansion and renovation of the Clinton clinic.
