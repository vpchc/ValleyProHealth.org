History of VPCHC

2008

Vermillion Parke Community Health Center establishes it's first clinic in Clinton.

2009

New VPCHC clinic opens in Cayuga.

2012

The MSBHC(Mobile School Based Health Center) opens.

2013

New VPCHC clinic opens in Bloomingdale.

2013

VPCHC received recognition through the National Committee for Quality Assurance (NCQA) as a Patient Centered Medical Home with a Distinction in Patient Experience Reporting.

2014

Vermillion Parke Community Health Center changes it's name to Valley Professionals Community Health Center.

2014

VPCHC names Terry Warren as new CEO.

2015

New VPCHC clinic opens in Crawfordsville.

2016

Cayuga clinic moves to new location in old Speedco building.

2016

VPCHC receives $1 million dollar grant from HRSA for expansion of Clinton clinic.
